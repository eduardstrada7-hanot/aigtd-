Privacy Policy — Bastion: Tower Defense

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Bastion is a single-player game that runs on your device.

Data we collect

We collect nothing ourselves. The developer does not collect, sell, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, and no servers run by us.

Advertising

Bastion shows optional rewarded video ads — ads you choose to watch in exchange for an in-game bonus — through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google/AdMob may collect and process certain data, including:

  • device and advertising identifiers (e.g. the Google Advertising ID);
  • coarse location and IP address;
  • app/device information and ad-interaction data.

This is processed by Google, not by us. You can review how Google uses this data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites, and you can reset or limit ad personalization in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking). On iOS the app asks for tracking permission, which you may decline.

Data stored on your device

The game saves your progress locally using standard on-device storage (the browser/WebView localStorage). This includes:

  • your best wave reached;
  • unlocked research, achievements, and tower skins;
  • custom maps you create in the level editor;
  • your settings (sound, music, chosen enemy faction).

This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can erase it at any time by clearing the app's data / storage, or by uninstalling the app.

Children's privacy

Bastion is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Third parties

Bastion uses Google AdMob to display ads (see "Advertising" above). It uses no other third-party tracking or analytics services.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
