How to play: Build towers beside the road to stop enemies from reaching your keep. Each leak costs lives (bosses cost 5). Earn gold from kills, wave bonuses and 5% interest on saved gold — and bonus gold for calling waves early.

Know your arsenal: Gunners shred swarms · Cannons splash grouped foes (can't hit flyers) · Frost slows everything nearby · Tesla chains through packs · Snipers pierce armor at huge range · Beacons buff every tower around them.

Know your enemy: Tanks shrug off weak hits (armor) · Flyers ignore Cannons · Healers mend the horde · Shields recharge — burst them down · Splitters break apart on death · Bosses bring everything.

Keys: 1–8 build · Space pause · F speed · N call wave · U upgrade · X sell · Esc cancel · Click a tower to inspect, set targeting, upgrade.
