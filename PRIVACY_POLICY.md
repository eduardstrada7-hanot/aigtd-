# Privacy Policy — Bastion: Tower Defense

_Last updated: 2026-06-19_

Bastion is a single-player game that runs entirely on your device.

## Data we collect

**None.** Bastion does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal
information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no
network requests to our servers — the game has no servers.

## Data stored on your device

The game saves your progress locally on your device using standard on-device
storage (the browser/WebView `localStorage`). This includes:

- your best wave reached,
- unlocked research and achievements,
- custom maps you create in the level editor,
- and your settings (sound, chosen enemy faction).

This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can
erase it at any time by clearing the app's data / storage, or by uninstalling the
app.

## Children's privacy

Bastion is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from
anyone, including children under 13.

## Third parties

Bastion includes no third-party advertising, tracking, or analytics services.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a
